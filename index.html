Hi. I'm Devendra Mandloi.

A passionate Interior Designer,
I hold a degree in Mechanical Engineering, a field that has equipped me with a strong foundation in 2D & 3D Drawings, problem-solving, analytical thinking, and attention to detail.
Despite my academic background, my true passion lies in the dynamic and ever-evolving realm of Interior Field.
I am driven by a deep curiosity and enthusiasm for creating functional and aesthetic furniture designs, and I am committed to leveraging my engineering skills to make a meaningful impact in the field of Design.
I specialize in handling loose furniture detailed drawings based on client requirements and ensure production-ready outputs with precision and quality. .
